Requirements

Functional Requirements

  • FR-1: The system shall allow organizers to create and define event details, including date, time, location, ticket types, prices, and capacity.
  • FR-2: Organizers can manage ticket sales in real-time and view details on the number of tickets sold and the remaining capacity.
  • FR-3: Organizers can list merchandise and concessions for sale within the event.
  • FR-4: Attendees can browse events by category, date, location, and popularity.
  • FR-5: The system shall allow attendees to purchase tickets for events and provide a unique QR code upon successful payment.
  • FR-6: Attendees can pre-order merchandise and concessions tied to their QR code for collection at the event.
  • FR-7: Attendees can use their QR code to check in at the event and collect pre-ordered items.
  • FR-8: Vendors can access and fulfill attendee orders by scanning QR codes.
  • FR-9: Vendors can manage and update their inventory in real time, displaying current stock levels to organizers and attendees.
  • FR-10: The system shall handle secure payments and process ticket, merchandise, and concession orders.
  • FR-11: The system shall generate an updated QR code reflecting any new orders placed during the event.
  • FR-12: Organizers can view analytics on ticket sales, attendee check-ins, merchandise sales, and vendor performance.
  • FR-13: The system shall provide post-event data insights for organizers, accessible through an analytics dashboard.

Non-Functional Requirements

  • NFR-1: The system shall handle high volumes of traffic, especially during peak times around event sales and check-ins.
  • NFR-2: Implement load balancing and auto-scaling for seamless performance across services.
  • NFR-3: The system shall implement role-based access control for organizers, attendees, and vendors.
  • NFR-4: Integrate secure payment gateways to process transactions.
  • NFR-5: Encrypt sensitive data, including payment details and personal information, both in transit and at rest.
  • NFR-6: Ensure 99.9% uptime, especially during high-traffic periods like event bookings and attendee check-ins.
  • NFR-7: Provide mechanisms for system recovery in the case of failures to maintain consistent user experience.
  • NFR-8: Ensure a responsive design that supports mobile and desktop devices for all users.
  • NFR-9: Provide intuitive user interfaces for organizers, attendees, and vendors, tailored to their respective roles and functions.
